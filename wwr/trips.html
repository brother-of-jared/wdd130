Featured Trips

Beginner’s Flow –
Cagayan Discovery Run

Perfect for families and first-time rafters, this half-day adventure takes you through gentle rapids surrounded by lush riverbanks and scenic mountain views. Our friendly guides ensure a fun, safe, and confidence-building experience for everyone.

The Explorer’s Rush –
Chico River Challenge

Experience the thrill of white-water action with this full-day adventure designed for those who crave excitement. Navigate class III–IV rapids, enjoy riverside lunch, and immerse yourself in the beauty of untouched nature.

Ultimate Quest Expedition –
Multi-Day Adventure

For the true adventurer, this two-day rafting expedition combines high-intensity rapids with peaceful campfire nights under the stars. Ideal for groups, teams, or those seeking the ultimate outdoor escape.
